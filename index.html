About me

I fell in love with the fitness and strength-training some-what later in life. It wasn't until my mid 20's that I started to take it seriously. I took the time to build endurance, gain muscle, and eventually competed in powerlifting on a local level. I remember what it was like not knowing what to do, or how to lift weights at all. I faced many many challenges and obstacles of adhering to programs and staying accountable. This is why, I want to work with YOU.

I believe that a healthy mind is a healthy body. A training regimen should be challenging but it should not run you into the ground. It took years until I recognized that more is not always better. The quality of your training and your life is what matters the most. I believe that taking care of your self outside of the gym is equally as important as the work you put in.

My training programs and nutrition advice is based on the principle that there are no quick fixes. There are no secet pills. Hard work over a long period of time is ultimately what will bring you the best results of your life. All training plans are customized to the client's desires, schedules, time constraints, & goals. None of my training programs are exactly the same.

Each training program is formulated based on an extensive questionnaire to be filled out by potential clients prior to working together.

Powerlifting meet prep is also an option when you work with me. Each client will have access to weekly check ins, email support, and and form analysis through me.

After sending in your inquiry, I will follow up with you in 24-48 hours to further discuss your goals and aspirations in depth. I will have a more detailed questionnaire to make sure we are a good fit to work with each other.
